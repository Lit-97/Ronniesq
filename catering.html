BBQ Catering Service in Middle Tennessee

Are you planning a big group event? Allow Ronnie's Q in Dickson to handle the menu! Whether you're organizing a family reunion, corporate event, wedding reception, or tailgate party, our BBQ catering is sure to impress your guests. Ronnie's Q offers a full catering menu packed with hickory-smoked favorites — including tender pulled pork, juicy chicken, fall-off-the-bone ribs, and flavorful brisket.

Our buffet-style catering includes everything you need: classic sides like baked beans, mac & cheese, and coleslaw, plus sweet tea and lemonade by the gallon. We’ll bring the buns, BBQ sauce, desserts, and all the disposable serving supplies — so you can relax and enjoy the event while we handle the food.

Looking for something smaller? Our Family Packs are the perfect option. Choose from our Feeds 5 or Feeds 10 meals, with your choice of smoked meats, two delicious sides, buns, BBQ sauce, and a gallon of sweet tea or lemonade. They’re great for a casual dinner at home, a picnic at Montgomery Bell State Park, or any last-minute get-together.

Ready to get started? Browse our catering options below or give us a call to discuss your event. At Ronnie’s Q, we bring the flavor of Dickson to every bite!
