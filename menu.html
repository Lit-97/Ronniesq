Hickory-Smoked BBQ Cooked Slow & Served Fast

At Ronnie’s Q in Dickson, we fire up the grill daily to bring you fresh, hickory-grilled pork, tender chicken, juicy ribs, and melt-in-your-mouth brisket — all served with your favorite fixings and classic Southern sides. Whether you're craving a hearty dinner plate or a combo meal, you’ll find satisfying options made the Ronnie’s Q way: slow-grilled, full of flavor, and never rushed. Round out your meal with sides like baked beans, slaw, potato salad, green beans, crispy French fries, fried okra, or a baked potato. Our BBQ is available by the half-pound or pound, or you can go big with a whole pork butt, half chicken, or whole chicken — perfect for feeding the family or saving for later. Sides can also be ordered in bulk (half-pint, pint, or quart), making it easy to bring the taste of Ronnie’s Q home. We also offer Family Packs built to feed either 5 or 10 people — complete with your choice of meat, 2 sides (per pack), buns, BBQ sauce, paper products, and a free jug of sweet tea, unsweet tea, or lemonade.

Take a look at our full menu below! Whether you're dining in or planning to feed a crowd, Ronnie’s Q serves up bold Southern flavor, real value, and a warm welcome every time.
